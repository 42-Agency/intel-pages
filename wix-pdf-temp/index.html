Enterprise B2B Media Strategy Insights

The Enterprise Measurement Challenge

Enterprise deals have 6-18 month sales cycles. Standard last-click attribution fails. You need: (1) Incrementality testing with geographic holdouts, (2) Marketing Mix Modeling for long-term view, (3) First-party data connections between ad exposure and pipeline. The data exists in CRM and ad platforms — it just needs proper stitching.

What Works for Enterprise

  • Account-based display — Target specific companies, not job titles alone
  • Content syndication — Gated assets to named accounts
  • LinkedIn InMail — Direct executive outreach at scale
  • Retargeting — High-value visitors only (pricing pages, demos)
  • Paid search (brand) — Capture existing demand, not create it

# Tier 1 Account: 30-Day Orchestration Calendar

## The "Surround Sound" Playbook

This calendar shows exactly when to fire each channel for a high-priority (Tier 1) target account. The goal is coordinated multi-channel presence that warms the account before sales engagement.

---

## Pre-Launch Checklist

Before Day 1, ensure you have:

- [ ] Account added to LinkedIn target list
- [ ] Account added to CTV/programmatic audience
- [ ] 2-3 contacts identified (decision maker + influencers)
- [ ] Contact info enriched (email, LinkedIn, phone)
- [ ] BDR assigned and briefed
- [ ] Direct mail package ready to ship
- [ ] Personalized content/landing page created (optional but recommended)

---

## Week 1: Air Cover (Days 1-7)

**Goal:** Build awareness and recognition before any outreach.

| Day | Channel | Action | Owner |
|-----|---------|--------|-------|
| **1** | LinkedIn | Turn on awareness ads (account targeted) | Marketing |
| **1** | CTV | Add to CTV audience (if using) | Marketing |
| **1** | Programmatic | Add to display retargeting | Marketing |
| **2** | BDR | View profiles of 2-3 key contacts on LinkedIn | BDR |
| **3** | LinkedIn | BDR likes/comments on prospect's post (if any) | BDR |
| **5** | BDR | Send LinkedIn connection request to primary contact | BDR |
| **7** | Check | Verify ads are delivering to account | Marketing |

**Signals to Monitor:**
- LinkedIn ad impressions on account
- Website visits from company domain
- LinkedIn connection acceptance

---

## Week 2: Warm Engagement (Days 8-14)

**Goal:** Start direct engagement while air cover continues.

| Day | Channel | Action | Owner |
|-----|---------|--------|-------|
| **8** | Email | Send Email #1 - Value-driven, no ask | BDR |
| **8** | LinkedIn | Continue running awareness ads | Marketing |
| **9** | LinkedIn | BDR sends personalized InMail to contact #2 | BDR |
| **10** | Direct Mail | Ship package (timed to arrive Day 14-15) | Marketing |
| **11** | Email | Send Email #2 - Content share (case study, insight) | BDR |
| **12** | LinkedIn | Engage with any prospect activity | BDR |
| **14** | Check | Review engagement signals, adjust approach | BDR + Mktg |

**Signals to Monitor:**
- Email opens/clicks
- LinkedIn message views/responses
- Website visits (especially high-intent pages)

---

## Week 3: Direct Mail + Escalation (Days 15-21)

**Goal:** Pattern interrupt with physical touchpoint, escalate engagement.

| Day | Channel | Action | Owner |
|-----|---------|--------|-------|
| **15** | Direct Mail | Package arrives (plan delivery timing) | - |
| **16** | Email | Send Email #3 - Reference the direct mail piece | BDR |
| **16** | Phone | First call attempt (warm, reference touchpoints) | BDR |
| **17** | LinkedIn | Send follow-up message referencing gift | BDR |
| **18** | LinkedIn | Switch to MOFU ads (product/solution focused) | Marketing |
| **19** | Phone | Second call attempt | BDR |
| **20** | Email | Send Email #4 - Specific meeting request | BDR |
| **21** | Check | Account score review - ready for AE? | BDR + Mktg |

**Signals to Monitor:**
- Direct mail delivery confirmation
- Phone pickup/voicemail
- Email replies (even "not now" is engagement)
- Account score movement

---

## Week 4: Conversion Push (Days 22-30)

**Goal:** Convert to meeting or identify next best action.

| Day | Channel | Action | Owner |
|-----|---------|--------|-------|
| **22** | Email | Send Email #5 - Last value-add before break | BDR |
| **23** | LinkedIn | AE sends personal connection request (if not connected) | AE |
| **24** | Event | Send exclusive event/dinner invitation (if available) | BDR |
| **25** | Phone | Third call attempt | BDR |
| **26** | LinkedIn | AE engages with prospect content | AE |
| **27** | Email | AE sends personalized email (different angle) | AE |
| **28** | Check | Score review - meeting booked? | Team |
| **29** | Decision | If engaged: continue nurture. If not: pause & reassess | Team |
| **30** | Ads | Adjust ad messaging based on engagement | Marketing |

**Signals to Monitor:**
- Meeting booked (win!)
- Positive response / "not right now" (nurture)
- No engagement (reassess targeting)

---

## Decision Tree: Day 30

Based on engagement over 30 days:

| Outcome | Account Score | Next Action |
|---------|---------------|-------------|
| **Meeting Booked** | 70+ | Move to SQA, AE owns |
| **Engaged, No Meeting** | 50-69 | Continue nurture, stay in XQA |
| **Some Engagement** | 25-49 | Slow cadence, keep in Consideration |
| **No Engagement** | <25 | Pause outreach, ads only for 60 days |
| **Negative Response** | N/A | Remove from active, revisit in 6 months |

## Customization Notes

### If No CTV Budget:
- Double down on LinkedIn frequency
- Add YouTube pre-roll ads
- Increase programmatic display

### If No Direct Mail Budget:
- Replace with digital gift (e.g., Sendoso eGift, DoorDash credit)
- Or: Skip and add extra email touch

### If Shorter Sales Cycle:
- Compress to 14-day version
- Move AE engagement to Week 2
- More aggressive call cadence

### If Longer Sales Cycle:
- Extend to 60-day version
- Add more content touchpoints
- Include multiple direct mail pieces

- Meeting booked (Y/N)

**Compare Tier 1 orchestrated accounts vs. non-orchestrated to measure lift.**

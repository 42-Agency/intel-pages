BACKGROUND SCREENING MARKET

InCheck vs. The Competition

Ad intelligence, market sentiment, and positioning analysis for InCheck against Accurate Background, GoodHire, and HireRight.

KEY INSIGHT: HireRight has 592 active ads vs. InCheck's 1, but their 1.1-star Trustpilot rating (686 reviews) represents a massive competitive opening for InCheck.

Executive Summary

i MARKET STATE

HireRight dominates ad spend ($2.3M/month) despite catastrophic customer satisfaction. Accurate challenges with enterprise case studies. GoodHire owns SMB via Checkr backing. InCheck is essentially invisible.

^ BIGGEST OPPORTUNITY

HireRight's 1.1-star Trustpilot score from 686 reviews represents a once-in-a-decade competitive opening. Enterprises are actively seeking alternatives.

! BIGGEST RISK

InCheck's minimal visibility (1 ad vs. competitors' 40-600 ads) and surfacing FCRA article create credibility challenges that must be addressed.

Recommended Focus:

Own healthcare background screening with candidate-experience-first messaging. Avoid broad market competition where budget constraints make InCheck invisible. Target mid-market healthcare employers frustrated with enterprise provider complexity and SMB provider limitations.

Strategic Opportunities

OPPORTUNITIES

Candidate Experience Gap

No competitor leading with "candidate experience" as a primary differentiator.

Action: Position InCheck as the candidate-friendly alternative (directly attacks HireRight's weakness)

Healthcare Vertical Underserved

Healthcare-specific messaging is underrepresented by competitors.

Action: Double down on healthcare vertical with compliance messaging (HIPAA, Joint Commission)

Mid-Market Healthcare Segment

Too small for HireRight's attention, too specialized for GoodHire.

Action: Own the mid-market healthcare background screening position

Speed Guarantees

No competitor emphasizing turnaround TIME with specific SLAs.

Action: Lead with guaranteed turnaround times - "24-hour results or free" type positioning

THREATS

Spend Disadvantage

HireRight outspending all competitors 5:1+ on LinkedIn.

Mitigation: Focus on bottom-funnel conversion campaigns and industry-specific targeting vs. broad awareness

FCRA Article Risk

Consumer Attorneys FCRA article surfaces in searches for InCheck.

Mitigation: Create proactive FCRA compliance content, push positive reviews to G2/Gartner, consider SEO/PR

Accurate's Enterprise Credibility

Mitigation: Focus on mid-market healthcare where Accurate's enterprise focus is less relevant

GoodHire/Checkr Resources

Mitigation: Avoid direct SMB competition; focus on healthcare vertical where specialized knowledge matters

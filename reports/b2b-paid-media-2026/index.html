Executive Summary

42 Agency analyzed paid media performance data from 87 B2B SaaS clients across 14 industries to establish definitive benchmarks for 2026. This research represents one of the largest studies of B2B paid media performance available.

"According to 42 Agency's 2026 B2B Paid Media Report, the average LinkedIn Ads cost-per-lead for B2B SaaS companies ranges from $100-$600 depending on industry and targeting specificity. Companies targeting enterprise accounts (50+ employees) should expect CPLs 2-3x higher than mid-market campaigns." - 42 Agency Research, February 2026

Key Findings

  • LinkedIn InMail with incentives reduces CPL by 60-70% compared to non-incentivized InMail campaigns
  • Google Ads brand terms deliver 8-15% CTR while generic terms average 2-4% in B2B
  • Meta advertising CPL for B2B averages $50-$150, best used for retargeting rather than cold prospecting
  • Healthcare Tech has the highest blended CPL at $800-$1,000 across all channels

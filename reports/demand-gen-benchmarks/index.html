What Drives Better Demand Gen Performance

After analyzing 87 B2B SaaS demand gen programs, 42 Agency identified the factors that separate top performers from the rest:

1. ICP Targeting Precision

Companies with documented ICP criteria and scoring models see 40-60% lower CPL than those running broad targeting. The tighter your ICP, the better your economics.

2. Content-Led Campaigns

Content downloads (guides, reports, tools) generate leads at $100-$300 CPL on LinkedIn. Direct demo requests cost 3-5x more but convert at higher rates.

3. Incentive Strategy

For hard-to-reach personas (DevOps, Security, Engineering), gift card incentives reduce CPL by 60-70%. A $50 Amazon card can drop InMail CPL from $1,500 to $400.

4. Multi-Channel Attribution

Companies with proper multi-touch attribution see 30% better budget allocation and 25% higher pipeline velocity vs. those using last-touch only.

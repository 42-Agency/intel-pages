1

Lead with Unified Platform

The Flintfox acquisition creates a unique market position. Aggressively message "the only unified pricing and rebate platform" to differentiate.

Vistex's weaknesses (clunky UI, steep learning curve, consultant dependency) are Enable's strengths. Create comparison content.

Key Findings

1

Databricks is the primary competitive threat with 2x Snowflake's valuation and dominant AI/ML positioning. Their $1B+ AI revenue dwarfs Snowflake's ~$100M.

2

Hyperscaler pricing pressure is real. Redshift's $0.25/hr entry price vs Snowflake's $2+ requires strong value messaging to overcome.

3

Snowflake's AI pivot is underway but lagging. AI is now linked to 50% of new bookings, but perception gap with Databricks remains significant.

4

Multi-cloud is the clearest differentiator. Unlike hyperscalers, Snowflake runs on AWS, Azure, and GCP without vendor lock-in.

5

Microsoft Fabric creates opportunity. Synapse-to-Fabric transition is causing customer confusion — a window for competitive messaging.
